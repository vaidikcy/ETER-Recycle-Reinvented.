How ÉTER Works

Three simple steps to make recycling effortless and rewarding

1

Snap & Analyze

Use your camera to take a photo of any item. Our AI instantly identifies it, removes the guesswork, and provides clear, local recycling rules.

2

Prep & Perform

Get a simple, visual guide on how to prepare the item—from rinsing a bottle to removing a label—ensuring your recycling is sorted correctly.

3

Track & Triumph

Every item you recycle correctly earns you ÉTER Points and unlocks badges. Watch your positive impact on the world grow in real-time.
